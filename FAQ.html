Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a donation on HopeLink?

Making a donation on HopeLink is simple! Just browse the causes you want to support, click the "Donate Now" button, enter your donation amount, and follow the secure payment process.

How does my donation help?

Your donation directly supports the cause you've chosen, whether it's disaster relief, education, healthcare, or other charitable initiatives. We ensure that 100% of your contributions reach the intended recipients.

Can I track how my donations are being used?

Yes! HopeLink provides regular updates on all campaigns, so you can see how your contributions are making a difference. You will receive reports and updates via email or directly through your HopeLink account.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, donations made through HopeLink are tax-deductible in many countries. Please check your local tax regulations or consult a tax advisor for more details.

Can I donate anonymously?

Absolutely! When making a donation, you can choose to remain anonymous by selecting the anonymous donation option during the checkout process.
